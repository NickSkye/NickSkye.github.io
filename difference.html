Compost tea is a liquid produced by leaching all of the good things from compost. This includes soluble nutrients, bacteria, fungi, protozoa, and nematodes. And why would you want this tea on your garden? The same way our bodies ingest certain compounds to lessen the incidents of disease, compost tea does the same thing.

Our goal is not only to enrich our clients’ enjoyment of their gardens, but to enrich the environment surrounding it.

NO LEAF BLOWERS

You won’t find any loud, wind-making leaf blowers when we take care of your yard.  Not only do they give off emissions, they also blow the buds and leaves right off your blooming flowers.  If there’s a pile of leaves that needs to be cleaned-up, we’ll use a rake.

NO LAWN MOWERS

With many residences looking for more drought-tolerant gardens, large grass lawns are becoming a thing of the past.  They require a lot of water, especially during summer months.  So if you have a lawn, we’ll show you a water-wise alternative and recommend removing your grass.

NO WEED-WHACKERS

We’re not a fan of weeds, nor weed-whackers for that matter.  So if we spot weeds in your yard, we will pull them out one at a time, hand by hand.  Weed-whackers tend to chop anything in their path, which could destroy precious flowers and blooming plants that aren’t meant to be cut down.

NO CHEMICALS

There are many chemicals found in many landscape maintenance routines, from algaecides to remove bacteria from ponds, to fungicides used to treat affected plants and flowers.  However, you won’t find any of those in RENEW’s natural nutrients.  All of our fertilizers are 100% organic and holistically created.

NO WATER WASTE

Designing with drought tolerant, hardy plants is the core of RENEW’s philosophy.  We group plants thoughtfully when selecting shrubs, ground covers, and perennials so they naturally grow together and use about the same amount of water.  Also, by routinely cultivating your soil, we’re able to improve its ability to resist evaporation and retain moisture.
