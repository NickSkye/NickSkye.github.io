RESEARCH
Landscape analysis and consultation.


Let us take a look around. We’ll get to know your yard, evaluate your challenges, and then our expert horticulturalists will provide you with helpful information and suggestions to improve the health of your landscape.

  • Initial meeting
  • Plant health analysis
  • Soil analysis
  • Irrigation inspection
  • Drainage inspection
  • Report & suggestions

RESTORE
Complete landscape design and creation.


Our designers take an innovative, ecologically-minded approach to restoring landscapes that have suffered from poor gardening. We design to reflect your unique lifestyle with vibrant, water-wise landscapes that’ll revitalize you and are also environmentally sustainable.

  • Repair leaking valves
  • Replace line breaks and leaks
  • Check for broken sprinkler heads
  • Adjust automatic controllers
  • Annuals replacement when needed
  • Remove stumps

REGENERATE
Organic maintenance and green recycling.


Healthy soil supports healthy plants—it’s the natural way to promote a beautiful, sustainable garden. Our customized, chemical-free organic maintenance and green recycling program will transform your landscape into a year-round lush oasis.

  • Natural pruning
  • Weed control & deadheading
  • Companion planting
  • Mulching & aeration
  • Pruning & fertilization
  • Integrated pest management
